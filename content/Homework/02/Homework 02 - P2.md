Vietname's beauty is once again in the global spotlight, with two of its most [iconic]{ /aɪˈkɑː.nɪk/} destinations earning international recognition as Hoi An and Da Nang have been named among the world's 51 most beautiful destinations this year by the UK-based magazine Time Out. Beyond its well-preserved historic architecture. Hoi An is known for its narrow streets lined with low-rise heritage houses and lanterns, giving the town a timeless feel.
What sets the [ancient]{/ˈeɪn.ʃənt/} town apart is how [heritage]{/*ˈher.ɪ.tɪdʒ/* } blends [seamlessly]{/ˈsiːm.ləs.li/} with daily life. Hoi An is not just a tourist [attraction]{ /əˈtræk.ʃən/},but a living community. 
More recently, Hoi An was also [recognised]{/ˈrek.əɡ.naɪzd/} by Booking.com as one of Vietnam's most welcoming destinations, as parts of its 14th [annual]{ /ˈæn.ju.əl/} Traveller Review Awards, based on visitor [feedback]{/ˈfiːd.bæk/}.
