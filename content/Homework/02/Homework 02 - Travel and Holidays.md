## Q1. If you could travel anywhere in the world, where would you go?

I want to go to **Switzerland**. I have seen a lot of videos and photos about the natural landscape — it looks impressive and beautiful. I would love to explore the Swiss Alps and step out of my comfort zone by trying skiing for the first time.

---

## Q2. What problems can tourists face when traveling abroad?

I think the first problem is language — different accents can be a real challenge for tourists. Another problem is cultural mismatch; we might accidentally do something rude without knowing it. Also, someone may try to rip you off if you are not careful."

---

## Q3. Why do people say traveling is one of the best ways to learn?

I totally agree with that. When you travel, you can meet a lot of people and learn something from them. Besides, you can broaden your horizons when you visit different places and learn more about different cultures. Traveling helps you experience different cultures firsthand, which is something you can never get from a textbook
