## Q1. Which family member do you feel closest to? Why?

My younger brother is my immediate family, and I am quite close with him. It might be because we are **close in age** and we have the same role model. We rarely fall out, and I think of him as my closest sibling.

---

## Q2. How many children would you like to have? Do you prefer boys or girls?

To be honest, I don't really like children. They are quite noisy for me. But if I have children in the future, I'd like to have twins — it's good to save time. As for boys or girls, I don't mind at all. I don't have a preference — both are good, they're my children."

---

## Q3. Is it okay to live together before you get married? Why or why not?

If my future spouse agrees, it's okay for me — whatever she wants! Of course, her family would need to agree too. I think it can be useful because if we fall out during that time, we can learn more deeply about each other.
