## Q1. Why is it important to learn a foreign language?

Learning a foreign language is important because it opens up more opportunities in the future. If we want to earn more money, we may need to work overseas or apply for international companies. In all likelihood, language skills will always be a plus point when you interview in the Vietnamese job market.

---

## Q2. What qualities make a good teacher?

In my opinion, a good teacher needs several important qualities. First, a good teacher should be [patient]{ /ˈpeɪ.ʃənt/} because students will feel scared or hesitant if the teacher becomes [irritable]{/ˈɪr.ə.t̬ə.bəl/}. They also need to have deep knowledge of their subject so that they can confidently explain it to their students. Finally, good [pedagogical]{/ˌped.əˈɡɑː.dʒɪkəl/} skills are [essential]{} because many people have knowledge, but without the ability to deliver it effectively, that knowledge cannot reach the students

---

## Q3. If you do not get a university degree, do you lose face with your family and friends?

In my opinion, it is completely normal nowadays — people do not even care much about degrees or certifications anymore. If you have a good life, you can learn from experience and earn money without violating the law. In all likelihood, practical experience will become more valuable than a university degree in the future. You can absolutely be proud of yourself!
