More than 22 percent of single people in Vietnam have no intention of getting married or having children. This group accounts for over one-fifth of the population, and the number is showing signs of increasing, particularly in urban areas.

The survey was conducted online with around "two hundred and fifty seven" thousand respondents aged 18 to 60 nationwide by the Center of Sociology and Environment under the Southern Institute of Social Sciences during the 2024-2025 period.

Among the participants, sixty three percent still expressed a desire to get married and have children.
