## Q1. How many hours a day do you spend on social media?

To be honest, I spend around two to three hours a day on social media. I usually open it first thing in the morning to check messages and the latest trending topics. Sometimes the algorithm keeps showing me interesting videos, so I lose track of time and scroll for too long. I know it's a bit much, so recently I've been trying to take a break from social media more often.

---

## Q2. Can you live without social media? What are some benefits of social media?

Honestly, I could live without it, but it would be quite difficult. Social media has many benefits — it helps me stay in touch with friends and family, follow the news, and learn new things for free. It's also useful for work and study, because many influencers and companies share helpful content there. So I don't want to quit completely; I just want to use it in a healthier, more balanced way.

---

## Q3. Do you think social media will become more important in the future?

Yes, in all likelihood social media will become even more important in the future. More and more businesses are using it to sell products and reach customers, and many people are now trying to monetize their accounts. On top of that, AI-generated content is growing very fast, which will change how we create and share things online. So I believe it will play an even bigger role in both work and daily life.
