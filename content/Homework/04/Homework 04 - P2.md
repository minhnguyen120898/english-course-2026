From Monday, June the 1st, [conventional]{/kənˈven.ʃən.əl/} [mineral]{ /ˈmɪn.ər.əl/} petrol has officially been withdrawn from the market.
Consumers are now using two types of fuel, E5 and E10 [biofuel]{ /ˈbaɪ.oʊˌfjuː.əl/} petrol, marking a new step in Vietnam's biofuel development roadmap. 
At several petrol stations in Hanoi on Monday, sales continue as normal with no signs of concern among consumers.
Many motorists said they had already been using E10 for nearly a month.
I have been using E10 for about half a month. My [vehicle]{/ˈviː.ə.kəl/} is till quite new and i have not noticed any issues so far. 
I have been using it for around half a month. I drive every day and refill [regularly]{/ˈreɡ.jə.lɚ.li/}. So far, I have not experienced any problems.
However, some consumers said they remain interested in how the new fuel performs with different vehicle models.
