## Q1. Why do people prefer motorbikes in Vietnam?

There are many reasons, but I think cost, convenience, and flexibility are the keys. In Vietnam, the cost of a motorbike is much cheaper than a car. Because the roads are narrow, it is more comfortable for motorbike riders than car drivers — motorbikes are simply more flexible in heavy traffic jam.

---

## Q2. Do you think the roads are good in Vietnam? Why or why not?

Vietnam is a developing country, so the roads are good enough in the cities. However, some smaller towns were not designed for the amount of traffic we have today — the roads there are often too narrow and cannot handle the traffic congestion during rush hour.

---

## Q3. What are the advantages and disadvantages of electric motorbikes?

Well, e-bikes are great here because they save a ton of money on gas and help with the city's pollution. But on the flip side, charging them outside big cities is still a headache, and you always have to worry about the battery getting ruined during flood season.
