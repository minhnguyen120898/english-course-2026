## 🎮 Warm Up — Last Letter Game

One student says a word; the next student must say a word that **starts with the last letter** of the previous word. You have **5 seconds** to respond. If you repeat a word or take too long, you're out. Keep going until one student is left.

> Example: **traffi*c*** → **commut*e*** → **escap*e*** → **eleganc*e*** → …

**Discussion to open:** What was the most interesting topic you learned in the first five lessons?

## 🗣️ Speaking Tip — Agree, Disagree, or In Between

In a discussion or debate, react before you give your reason:

- ✅ **I agree** → _"I agree because…"_
- ❌ **I disagree** → _"I don't really agree because…"_
- 🤝 **In between** → _"That's true, but…"_

> These keep the conversation natural and polite, even when you don't share the same opinion.

## 💬 Discussion Questions

**Family**

1. What is the most important thing you must do to keep your family close?
2. Should young people move out and live far from their parents when they turn 18? Why or why not?

**Travel and Holidays**

3. Do you think taking a vacation is necessary? Why or why not?
4. Why do some people never travel?
5. Would you rather take a holiday or spend the same amount of money on something else?

**Food and Health**

6. Have you ever been disappointed by food that had a high rating?
7. What does "a balanced diet" mean to you?
8. Do you think people are becoming healthier or unhealthier?

**Transport and Traffic**

9. What are the main causes of traffic jams?
10. **Debate:** Motorbikes vs cars — which is better in big cities? / Public transport vs private vehicles — which should people use more?

**Education and Learning**

11. What are the advantages of studying abroad?
12. Are you planning to pursue higher education? Why?
13. Why do many people only start learning spoken English when they begin working or go to university, instead of starting earlier?
