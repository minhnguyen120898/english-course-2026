# 📚 Lesson 03 — Food and Health

## 📖 Vocabulary

### 1. Balanced Diet

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A diet that includes the right amounts of different food types to keep you healthy.
- **Example:** _"The doctor advised her to eat a balanced diet with more vegetables and less sugar."_

---

### 2. Protein / Vitamins / Minerals

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:**
    - **Protein** — a nutrient that builds and repairs muscles and body tissue. Found in meat, eggs, beans.
    - **Vitamins** — nutrients your body needs in small amounts to function properly. Found in fruits and vegetables.
    - **Minerals** — natural substances like calcium and iron that your body needs to stay healthy.
- **Example:** _"Athletes need a lot of protein to build muscle, as well as vitamins and minerals to stay healthy."_

---

### 3. Portion

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** The amount of food served or eaten at one time.
- **Example:** _"The restaurant portions were huge — I couldn't finish my meal."_

---

### 4. Hydration

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** The process of drinking enough water to keep your body healthy.
- **Example:** _"Good hydration is essential, especially when you exercise or stay out in the sun."_

---

### 5. Intermittent Fasting

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A diet pattern where you eat only during certain hours of the day and avoid food the rest of the time.
- **Example:** _"He tries intermittent fasting — he only eats between 12pm and 8pm every day."_

---

### 6. Food Poisoning

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** An illness caused by eating contaminated or spoiled food.
- **Example:** _"She got food poisoning after eating at that restaurant and had to stay in bed for two days."_

---

### 7. Guilty Pleasure

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Something you enjoy but feel slightly bad about, usually unhealthy food.
- **Example:** _"Fried chicken is my guilty pleasure — I know it's not healthy but I can't resist it!"_

---

### 8. Low-fat / High-fat

- **Type:** (adj)
- **Meaning:** Describing food that contains a small or large amount of fat.
- **Example:** _"He switched to low-fat milk after his doctor warned him about his cholesterol."_

---

### 9. Oily / Greasy

- **Type:** (adj)
- **Meaning:** Food that contains a lot of oil or fat, often leaving a shiny or slippery feeling.
- **Example:** _"The pizza was so greasy that the box was soaked through with oil."_

---

### 10. Mouthwatering

- **Type:** (adj)
- **Meaning:** Food that looks or smells so good it makes you want to eat it immediately.
- **Example:** _"The smell of fresh bánh mì in the morning is absolutely mouthwatering."_

---

### 11. Cut Down On

- **Type:** (phrasal verb)
- **Meaning:** To reduce the amount of something, especially something unhealthy.
- **Example:** _"My doctor told me to cut down on sugar and processed foods."_

---

### 12. Burn Off Calories

- **Type:** (v)
- **Meaning:** To use up energy from food through exercise or physical activity.
- **Example:** _"I went for a long run to burn off the calories from last night's dinner."_

---

### 13. Hit the Spot

- **Type:** (idiom)
- **Meaning:** When food or drink is exactly what you needed and satisfies you perfectly.
- **Example:** _"That cold iced coffee really hit the spot on such a hot afternoon."_

---

### 14. Comfort Food

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Food that makes you feel happy or better emotionally, usually familiar and filling.
- **Example:** _"After a bad day at work, I always crave comfort food like instant noodles or rice porridge."_

---

### 15. Faint

- **Type:** (v / adj)
- **Meaning:** To suddenly lose consciousness briefly, often due to hunger, heat, or low blood pressure.
- **Example:** _"She skipped breakfast and fainted during the morning meeting."_

---

### 16. Iron

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A mineral found in food (like red meat and spinach) that helps carry oxygen in your blood. Low iron causes tiredness and weakness.
- **Example:** _"She was told to eat more iron-rich foods because she felt tired all the time."_

---

### 17. Supplements

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Pills or powders that provide extra vitamins, minerals, or nutrients your diet may be missing.
- **Example:** _"He takes vitamin D and omega-3 supplements every morning with breakfast."_

---

### 18. Sunbathe

- **Type:** (v)
- **Meaning:** To sit or lie in the sun, usually to get a tan or absorb vitamin D.
- **Example:** _"She spent the afternoon sunbathing on the beach but forgot to wear sunscreen."_

---

### 19. Cartilage

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A firm but flexible tissue in your body found in joints, ears, and nose. It protects your bones.
- **Example:** _"He injured his cartilage while playing football and needed months of recovery."_
