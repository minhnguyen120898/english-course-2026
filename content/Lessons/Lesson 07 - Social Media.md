## 📖 Vocabulary

### 1. Post

- **Type:** (n / v)
- **Meaning:** Content you share online (photo, video, text); or the act of sharing it.
- **Example:** _"I just posted a new photo. Go check it out!"_

---

### 2. Emoji

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A small picture or icon used to show feelings or ideas in a message.
- **Example:** _"Why did you send that emoji? Are you joking?"_

---

### 3. Take a Break from Social Media

- **Type:** (phr. v)
- **Meaning:** To stop using social media for a while to rest your mind.
- **Example:** _"I need to take a break from social media. It's too stressful."_

---

### 4. Trending Topic

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A subject that suddenly becomes very popular and is discussed by many people online.
- **Example:** _"Did you see that? It's a trending topic right now."_

---

### 5. Algorithm

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** The system an app uses to decide what content to show you.
- **Example:** _"The algorithm keeps showing me the same type of videos."_

---

### 6. Clickbait

- **Type:** (v / n)
- **Meaning:** A title or thumbnail designed to trick you into clicking, often exaggerated or misleading.
- **Example:** _"That title is just trying to clickbait people."_

---

### 7. Rage Bait

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Content made on purpose to make people angry so they comment and share it.
- **Example:** _"Don't comment on that post. It's just rage bait."_

---

### 8. Influencer

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** A person with many followers who can affect others' opinions or buying habits.
- **Example:** _"She's an influencer, so she gets paid to promote products."_

---

### 9. Ghost Someone

- **Type:** (v)
- **Meaning:** To suddenly stop replying to someone with no explanation.
- **Example:** _"Why did you ghost me? I was waiting for your reply!"_

---

### 10. Flex

- **Type:** (v)
- **Meaning:** To show off something (money, looks, things) to impress others.
- **Example:** _"He's always flexing his new phone online."_

---

### 11. FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** The worried feeling that others are having fun or experiences without you.
- **Example:** _"I didn't go to the party, but now I have FOMO."_

---

### 12. Monetize

- **Type:** (v)
- **Meaning:** To earn money from something, like a channel or account.
- **Example:** _"He's trying to monetize his TikTok account."_

---

### 13. In-app Purchase

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Something you buy with real money inside an app or game.
- **Example:** _"I spent too much money on in-app purchases in that game."_

---

### 14. Censorship

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** The removal or blocking of content considered unacceptable.
- **Example:** _"Some content is removed because of censorship rules."_

---

### 15. AI-generated Content

- **Type:** (n)
- **Meaning:** Text, images, or video created by artificial intelligence rather than a person.
- **Example:** _"It's hard to tell if that video is real or AI-generated content."_

## 🔊 Pronunciation Practice — "ex-" at the start of words

Vietnamese speakers often say **/es/** → _ess-ample, ess-actly._ ❌

The correct sound is **"ex-" = /ɪgz/** (a soft **g + z** sound):

| Say it like… | Word       | IPA              |
| ------------ | ---------- | ---------------- |
| ig-ZAM-ple   | example    | /ɪɡˈzæmpl/       |
| ig-ZACT-ly   | exactly    | /ɪɡˈzæktli/      |
| ig-ZPECT     | expect     | /ɪkˈspekt/       |
| ig-ZPLAIN    | explain    | /ɪkˈspleɪn/      |
| ig-ZPRESS    | express    | /ɪkˈspres/       |
| ig-ZPENSIVE  | expensive  | /ɪkˈspensɪv/     |
| ig-ZPERI-ence| experience | /ɪkˈspɪəriəns/   |

## 🗣️ Speaking Tip — "Okie dokie"

> A very casual, friendly way to **agree, accept, or move on**.

- _"Let's meet at 6." → "Okie dokie."_
- _"Can you send it later?" → "Okie dokie, no problem."_
- _"Okie dokie — let's move on to the next point."_

## 💬 Discussion / Practice Questions

1. How many hours a day do you spend on social media?
2. Can you live without social media? What are some benefits of social media?
3. Generally speaking, is social media good or bad for society?
4. Does social media content influence your online purchases?
5. Before making a purchase, do you trust online reviews?
6. Have you ever ghosted someone on social media?
7. Is social media more useful for communication or entertainment?
8. Do you think social media will become more important in the future?
